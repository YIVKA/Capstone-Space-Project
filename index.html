Planets in our Solar System



There are eight different planets in our solar system.

Read this webpage to learn about them!



Mercury

Mercury is the first planet in the solar system.

That means it is the closest to the sun. It is the

fastest planet to revolve around the sun in our solar system.

It's shortest year is 88 days!



Venus

Venus is the second planet in the solar system.

It is the hottest planet in the solar system! A day on

Venus is a whole year!



Earth

Earth is the third planet in the solar system.

It is the planet we live on! Earth is the only

planet that produces life in the whole solar system.



Mars

Mars is the fourth planet in the solar system.

It is the second smallest planet behind the first

planet, Mercury.

Jupitar

Jupiter is the fifth planet in the solar system.

It is the largest planet in the solar system too.

Jupiter also has 80 moons!



Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet in the solar system.

It is famous for its ring around it. Its ring is made up

of rocks, minerals, and other solids.



Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet in the solar system.

It is the coldest planet in the solar system too.

Uranus also rotates from his side, unlike the

other planets.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and final planet in the

solar system. It is 58 times larger and 17 times heavier

than the Earth.
